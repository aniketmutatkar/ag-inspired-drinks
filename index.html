A Cocktail Built From The Farms

The Verdant
Harvest

A bourbon cocktail built from the farms: a milk clarification of pistachio orgeat and sous vide spiced bourbon. Made for the people who feed everyone else.

The story

I wanted to make something special for our virtual happy hour, not just crack open a beer and call it a day. We spend all year working with the people who grow the food this country runs on, so it felt right to build a drink that actually reflects that.

Every ingredient in this cocktail ties back to a real California agricultural crop, the kind grown by the farmers and growers that UnitedAg has been supporting for over 45 years.

🌿

Made with Setton Pistachio

The pistachio orgeat is built around Setton Farms pistachios, a grower based in California's Central Valley. California produces over 98% of the US pistachio supply. Setton is one of the largest pistachio processors in the world.

The milk clarification is the fun part. The drink goes in looking like a murky bourbon situation and comes out crystal clear, pale gold, silky, with this ghost of pistachio and spice. It sounds insane. During the clarification process, the milk proteins bind to tannins and color compounds and pull them out through the cheesecloth. You're left with all the flavor and none of the cloudiness.

How it's made

Three techniques, one drink

This isn't a "pour bourbon over ice" situation. There are three distinct processes happening, each building on the last. Here's the breakdown.

01

Pistachio Orgeat

Raw pistachios toasted, blended into milk, strained through cheesecloth, and sweetened. Replaces commercial orgeat entirely: fresher, nuttier, and you actually know what's in it.

02

Sous Vide Infusion

Bourbon with cardamom, cinnamon, and orange peel sealed in a mason jar and cooked at 135°F for 90 minutes. What would take 5 days of cold steeping takes 90 minutes.

03

Milk Clarification

The assembled cocktail is curdled with whole milk, rested, then filtered through cheesecloth. The curds strip color and tannins, leaving a clear, silky drink with all the flavor intact.

The recipe

The Verdant Harvest

Bourbon · Pistachio Orgeat · Spiced · Clarified

Serves 1–2

Ingredients

Pistachio Orgeat

Raw pistachiosSetton Farms preferred 1/2 cup
Warm water 3/4 cup
White sugar 1/2 cup
Vanilla extract 1/4 tsp

Bourbon Infusion

BourbonElijah Craig or Knob Creek 6 oz
Cardamom podscracked open 4 pods
Cinnamon stick 1
Orange peelno white pith 1/2 orange

Cocktail + Clarification

Infused bourbon 4 oz
Pistachio orgeat 1 oz
Fresh lemon juice 3/4 oz
Honeythinned with a few drops water 1/4 oz
Whole milkfor clarification only 3/4 oz
Orange peelexpressed, garnish 1 strip

Method

1

Toast pistachios at 325°F for 10 min. Pull when you smell them, don't burn them.

2

Blend toasted pistachios with 3/4 cup warm water for 60 seconds. Strain through cheesecloth, squeeze hard. Combine liquid with sugar + vanilla over low heat until dissolved. Cool completely.

3

Sous vide bourbon + cardamom + cinnamon + orange peel in a sealed mason jar at 135°F for 90 minutes. Strain through cheesecloth after. Let cool.

4

Combine in a pitcher: 4 oz infused bourbon, 1 oz pistachio orgeat, 3/4 oz lemon juice, 1/4 oz thinned honey. Stir.

5

Clarify: slowly pour 3/4 oz whole milk into the cocktail mixture. It will curdle immediately. Leave it completely undisturbed for 15 minutes.

6

Strain twice through doubled cheesecloth. Do not rush or squeeze. Second pass should be clear. You now have a crystal clear cocktail.

7

Serve in a chilled coupe, no ice. Express orange peel over the glass and drop it in. Optionally, crush 3 pistachios and dust the rim.

Tasting notes

Warm spice and cardamom up front, followed by a creamy pistachio sweetness. The bourbon backbone comes through clean. Silky texture from the clarification. The orange peel oil on the surface ties everything together on the finish.
